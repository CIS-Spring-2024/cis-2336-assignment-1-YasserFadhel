YASSER FADHEL

A passionate food enthusiast who established CoogEats, a bustling cafeteria in Sugar Land, to cater to the University of Houston's community.
Driven by his love for cooking and a desire to create a vibrant dining experience, Yasser curated a menu filled with tasty dishes inspired by local flavors.

At CoogEats, Yasser emphasizes using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to ensure every meal is packed with flavor and quality.
He believes in the power of good food to bring people together and is committed to providing a welcoming environment for students, faculty, and locals alike.
